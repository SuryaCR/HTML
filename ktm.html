THE THRILL CHASER


KTM 250 DUKE

If chasing thrills is your game then climb aboard the KTM 250 DUKE. It combines maximum riding exhilaration with optimum user value and is hard to beat wherever nimble handling counts. Lightweight, powerful and packed with advanced technology, it promises to deliver a thrilling ride from sunrise to sunset. At First you notice that aggressive front end. that predatory, READY TO RACE stance. When you come closer, you feel its steel fuel tank and multiple high-quality cast parts.




1. ENGINE

The latest materials and production technologies not only deliver the bike's bountiful torque and punchy acceleration, but also good manners in everyday use and outstanding fuel economy. A bike as compact and nimble as this one deserves to pack a solid punch. With 22 kW (30 hp) for an extremely lightweight craftsmanship, it does just that. Thanks to the ingenious intake and exhaust system, the 38mm throttle body and the meticulous fuel injection system.




2. CHASSIS

The KTM 250 DUKE comes as standard with ultra-lightweight, high- performance upside-down WP forks. Using the latest open-cartridge technology, these forks not only perform above expectations when pushed to the limits, but are also nice and easy to maintain. This makes them perfect for all aspects of riding, from relaxed cruising to hammering around on closed circuits and everthing in between.




3. BODYWORK & GRAPHICS

Atfirst you notice that aggresive front end. That predatory, Ready to race stance. when you come closer, you feel its steel fuel tank and multiple high-quanlity cast parts. In the end , you realize that the design and production standards of this bike were set sky-high. The LEDs in Headlight produces outstanding visibility for riding in low light or dark conditions, the parking lights, which consists of 20 LEDs, ensures that the bike is highly visible to other road users. A 100% digital LCD display has everthing you would expect from a state of the art KTM.
